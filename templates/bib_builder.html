Tips for cleaner output

  • One entry per paragraph. Blank lines between entries help, but the parser will also split on lines that begin with a capitalized surname + comma.
  • Italic book or journal titles in your source come through best when they survive the paste — Word and Google Docs both export asterisks if you paste as Markdown. If your source is plain text, wrap titles in *asterisks* by hand for accuracy.
  • For MLA repeat-author entries (---.), the parser carries the previous entry's authors forward automatically.
  • Anything the parser couldn't classify drops into the entry's note field so the citation isn't lost.
  • The output is a starting point — give it a quick read before using it. Pandoc will surface any BibTeX syntax errors on the next render.
